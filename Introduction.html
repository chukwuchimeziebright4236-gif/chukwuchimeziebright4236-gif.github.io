Introduction

This book is written to expose the six weapons of God we can use to contend against principalities, against powers, against the world rulers of this present darkness, and against the spiritual hosts of wickedness in high places. In Ephesians 6:12, Paul wrote, “For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places.”

First of all, Paul was speaking to the Church in Ephesus. In verse 11 of Ephesians 6, he said, “Put on the whole armor of God, that ye may be able to stand against the wiles of the devil.” He was letting them know that our battle is not against flesh and blood.

Consequently, he began revealing the weapons of God they could use to fight these spiritual powers and rulers, enabling them to stand against the wiles of the devil. This message also reveals to us the weapons of God required to fight and win in this spiritual warfare. Even though we start from a position of victory—because Jesus already defeated them and made a public spectacle of the devil in hell—we must still stand.

Paul did not reveal these weapons based on his own intellect, but by the inspiration of the Holy Spirit. Therefore, reader, I want you to pay close attention to these principles and painstakingly read through them all. If you know and understand these truths, you will reign in life and have dominion over the world. Remember what Jesus said to His disciples: “And these signs shall follow them that believe; In my name shall they cast out devils…” However, dealing with these other powers of darkness and stopping their operations requires more than just a casual mention; it requires the authority found only in the name of Jesus Christ.

Consider principalities: these are powers that rule over a specific country, state, or region.

Then there are the rulers of this present darkness: these are spiritual legislators. They decree laws that govern a family, community, state, country, continent, or even the entire world. To back these laws, they utilize enforcement agents. Demons travel to and fro to ensure their spiritual laws are maintained, functioning much like the modern police and military forces we see today. They ensure that lawbreakers are punished based on what is written in their spiritual decrees. For instance, if the world rulers of this present darkness legislate that no one can become president unless they are corrupt, they will fight aggressively to see that law fulfilled.

This leads to the next level of authority: the spiritual hosts of wickedness. These groups host their meetings in heavenly places. It is from this realm that they look for human vessels willing to align with them to manifest their devilish wiles here on Earth. They are relentlessly determined.

So, stop complaining about the injustices you see today. Stop complaining that the world is wicked; the earthly realm is simply where decisions made in the spirit are executed. What happens on Earth is the direct result of decisions taken in the spiritual realm. That is why these weapons are vital for our dominion on this Earth—to ensure that Christ always reigns in our hearts, our families, our states, our nations, our continents, and our world.

The armor and weapons of God for spiritual warfare are: Truth, Righteousness, Peace, Faith, Salvation, and the Word of God (which is the sword of the Spirit).

As Ephesians 6:14-17 states: “Stand therefore, having your loins girt about with truth, and having on the breastplate of righteousness; And your feet shod with the preparation of the gospel of peace; Above all, taking the shield of faith, wherewith ye shall be able to quench all the fiery darts of the wicked. And take the helmet of salvation, and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God.”
